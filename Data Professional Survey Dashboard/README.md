# Data Professional Survey Breakdown | Power BI

## Objective
To analyse survey responses from data professionals to understand salary trends, job roles, programming preferences, demographics, and job satisfaction levels.

## Dataset
- Source: Data Professional Survey Dataset
- Contains survey responses including country, job title, salary, age, gender, programming language preference, and satisfaction scores.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel

## Key Analysis
- Survey participants by country
- Average salary by job title
- Salary comparison by gender
- Favourite programming languages among data professionals
- Work-life balance and salary satisfaction scores

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards showing total survey respondents and average age
- Treemap for country-wise respondent distribution
- Bar chart for average salary by job title
- Donut chart for salary distribution by gender
- Gauge charts for work-life balance and salary satisfaction

## Business Insights
- Data Analysts and Data Engineers represent a significant portion of survey respondents.
- Python is the most preferred programming language among data professionals.
- Salary satisfaction is lower compared to work-life balance satisfaction, indicating compensation concerns.
- Insights can help organisations understand market expectations and improve talent attraction strategies.

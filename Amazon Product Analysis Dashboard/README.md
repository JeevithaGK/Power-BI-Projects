# Amazon Product Performance Analysis | Power BI

## Objective
To analyze Amazon product data and evaluate product performance across categories using ratings, reviews, and discount patterns in order to support data-driven business decisions related to pricing and promotions.

## Dataset
- Source: Amazon Product Dataset (Kaggle)
- Includes product category, ratings, rating counts, discount percentages, and review volume.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel

## Key Analysis
- Average rating by product category
- Total products and reviews by category
- Relationship between ratings and rating count
- Discount patterns across different categories

## Dashboard Features
- Category-wise slicer for interactive filtering
- KPI cards showing total products, average rating, average discount, and total reviews
- Bar charts for category-level performance
- Scatter plot to analyse rating vs rating count

## Business Insights
- Electronics and Home & Kitchen categories have the highest product count and reviews.
- High discounts do not always result in higher ratings, indicating quality and customer experience play a key role.
- Categories with consistent ratings and high review volumes represent stable business segments.
- Insights can help businesses optimize discount strategies and focus on high-performing categories.
